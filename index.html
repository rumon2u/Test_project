The power of Squarespace, now on the go.

Squarespace does more than help you build beautiful websites. From concept to execution, our apps help you manage your ideas and get them online. And now they're even more accessible than ever.

Go mobile with Squarespace Apps.

Portfolio. Blog. Note. Metrics. They're more than apps, they are extensions of your website that increase productivity on the go. Now you can leave your laptop at home and make updates right from the palm of your hand.

Blog

Designed for bloggers on the go, Squarespace Blog lets you write and edit blog posts from anywhere.

Note

Send notes to yourself via e-mail or sync with a range of popular services including Evernote, Dropbox, and Google Drive.

Metrics

Metrics is a fluid way to monitor your website's analytics from page views to unique visitors, projections, social media follower counts and more.

Portfolio

Portfolio synchronizes with all the galleries on your Squarespace website, letting you showcase your work on a mobile device without relying on an internet connection.

Squarespace apps come to Android.

We waited until we could do it right. Then we did. Instead of creating a carbon copy of our popular iOS Note and Blog apps, we've taken advantage of the Android platform in a way that creates a rich new experience for Android users.
